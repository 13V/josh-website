A little about us

Built by enthusiasts,
for enthusiasts

JTR Garage started the same way most good things in this scene do — a couple of mates, a shed in Adelaide, and a serious problem with anything that's turbocharged, rear-wheel drive, and built properly. We grew up on the legends: the R34s and GT-Rs that earned the name Godzilla, the HSVs that ruled the Aussie streets. Today we hunt down, prep and sell the cars we'd happily keep for ourselves.

We're not a faceless dealer flogging stock. Every car on our floor is one we'd put our own name to — honest listings, real history, no surprises. And because the build never really stops, we make Japanese-inspired streetwear for the people who get it: clean, quality gear that says where you're from without saying a word. Cars and culture, straight out of South Australia.

From the founder

A note from Joshua

It started for me as a kid — catching sight of an R33 GTR rolling past and never quite getting over it. That sound, those lines, the idea that a car could be that serious. The obsession with 90s JDM never faded; it just turned into a business. JTR Garage is me trying to share that feeling with people who already understand it. No sales pitch, no nonsense — just the cars and the culture I'd geek out over anyway.

"We're not above the culture — we're in it."
