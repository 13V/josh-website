FAQs

Quick answers

Shipping +
Free shipping Australia-wide on apparel orders over $100. Everything ships from our base in Adelaide, with tracking sent to your email once it's on the way. Most metro orders land within a few business days.
Returns & refunds +
We offer 30-day returns on unworn apparel with tags attached, on top of your rights under the Australian Consumer Law. If something's faulty or not as described, we'll sort it out properly — just get in touch and we'll make it right.
Sizing +
Our tees and hoodies are cut for an oversized, street fit. If you prefer a regular fit, size down one. Specific measurements are listed on each product page — when in doubt, send us a message and we'll point you the right way.
Vehicle inspections +
Inspections are welcome by appointment here in Adelaide — bring your mechanic, bring a mate, have a proper look. Interstate buyer? No worries. We can arrange transport Australia-wide and walk you through every detail beforehand.
Do you buy cars? +
Yes — if you've got the right kind of JDM or AUDM metal, we'd love to hear about it. Use the contact form above and choose "Sell my car" as the subject. Tell us the chassis, the history and where it's at, and we'll be in touch.
