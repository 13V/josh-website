Less is more, and the AE86 proved it. No turbo, no all-wheel drive, no electronics to save you — just a featherweight body, a rev-happy 4A-GE up front and rear-wheel drive sending it all to the back. The Hachiroku was the underdog that out-handled cars with twice the power, and it became the patron saint of touge running in the process. The 86 Legacy Tee carries that icon across the back in a clean Japanese-inspired design.

Cut from premium heavyweight 260gsm 100% cotton with a comfortable oversized fit, it's a tribute to balance over brute force — and to every enthusiast who knows that grip, weight and a good corner beat a dyno number any day. Built to be worn, not framed.
